A Culture of Exploration

The thriving metropolis, Pune stores many tourist places that often entice the visitors. With a splash of old world charm, Pune has a number of places of historical importance; and is also lined with other modern attractions like gardens and temples. It is one of those cities that must be visited during Maharashtra tours as it perfectly reflects the state’s diversity.
Short vacations bring about family to some of Pune’s popular tourist places such as the Chaturshringi temple of Goddess Ambareshwari, Ambedkar museum, Parvati temple, Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park,Dagaduseth Halwai Ganpati temple, rock carved Pataleshwar temple, Aga khan palace where Mahatma Gandhi was kept under house arrest and the famous Shaniwar wada built during the time of Bajirao Peshwa.
